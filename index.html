Features

Report Lost Items

Submit details and photos of items you’ve lost around campus.

How It Works

  1. Register or Login with your student account.
  2. Report lost or found items with details and photos.
  3. Browse items and claim what belongs to you.
  4. Track your reports and claims in your dashboard.
